Leonardo March is a freelance visual journalist based in Brooklyn, New York. Currently a part-time contributor to The Haitians Times, Leonardo works with photos, videos and some graphics telling stories about the Haitian diaspora in New York City, mainly.

Originally from Puerto Rico, Leonardo has made the neighborhood of Flatbush, in Brooklyn, his home.
